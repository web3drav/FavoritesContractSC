# FavoritesContractSC
Source code to deploy your own Favorites Contract on Solana




1. Go to https://beta.solpg.io


2.import the lib.rs and click deploy.

3. after deployement is successful, click the build button.

4. go to the section that looks like a test tube.


5. click on setFavorites


6. then enter your favorites from color to hobbies etc. after you did it

user: current wallet favorites: from Seed, -> string= "favorites" . (custom), add seed--> current wallet.

7. Then hit the generate button!

after you're done you can click "TEST"


after you clicked "TEST" you will get an notification with the link to the solana explorer.
when you scroll to the bottom, you can find a log where you will see your favorite color, hobbies etc you entered before.
